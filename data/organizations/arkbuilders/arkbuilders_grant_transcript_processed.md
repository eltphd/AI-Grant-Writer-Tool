## Program Description

### Target Population
- Fathers
- Grandfathers  
- Future fathers
- **Intergenerational approach** - connecting elders, fathers, and youth

### Program Goals
- **Empower men to reach men** - Men-led mental health support
- Reduce mental health stigma in faith communities
- Create intergenerational conversations
- Build community of practice

### Key Activities

1. **Barbershop Partnerships**
   - Identify and partner with 5-8 barbershops
   - Men-to-men mental health conversations
   - Outreach and engagement

2. **Train-the-Trainer Model**
   - Train elders, fathers, and young people
   - Ending the Silence training
   - QPR (Question, Persuade, Refer) training
   - Mental health stigma reduction for faith community elders

3. **Community Conversations**
   - "If adults would listen, what would you tell them?" (youth)
   - "If kids would listen, what would you tell them?" (elders)
   - Social perspective-taking activities

4. **Community of Practice Development**
   - Convene leaders from faith community, elders, fathers, and youth
   - Develop sustainable model for replication

## Key Program Elements

### Men-to-Men Approach
- Critical that men train and support men
- Women cannot effectively facilitate men's mental health groups
- Empowers men to "own their own" mental health support

### Intergenerational Focus
- Connects grandfathers, fathers, and future fathers
- Bridges wisdom of elders with needs of youth
- Creates mutual understanding and support

### Faith Community Integration
- Addresses mental health stigma in faith communities
- Trains faith leaders to support men's mental health
- Leverages existing community structures

### Pilot Model
- Designed as pilot for replication
- Foundation for larger funding requests
- Develops sustainable community of practice

## Partnership Structure

- **NAMI Franklin County:** Fiscal agent, receives funds, distributes to partners
- **NAMI ArkBuilders:** Technical assistance, training, program implementation
- **NAMI Allen County:** Local implementation partner

### Workflow
1. NAMI Franklin County receives grant funds
2. Takes 10% administrative fee
3. Distributes remaining funds to partners
4. ArkBuilders provides TA/training services
